"RESEA'S objective is to rid the ocean from all the plastic it is filled with, and recycle it making them into cute and reusable plastic bags, that can be used for many things"

Scientific justification

The effects of consumer waste that pollutes the water will continue to rise and negatively impact the ecosystem. that's why it is important to recycle and the care of the ocean.

Materials Used

  • - Hot Iron
  • - Scissors
  • - Plastic Bags
  • - Wax Paper
  • - Thick Ribbon/Strip

Methodology

Step 0

Gather all the materials needed together, these were listed above.

Step 1

Cut out several squares from the plastic bags you've collected.

Step 2

Place wax paper on top of the plastic until it joins your pieces through melting.

Step 3

Using a hot iron, shape the plastic into a bag.

Step 4

Finally, give the bag a thick strip that will act as a bag sleeve.

Step 5

Delight in its use!
